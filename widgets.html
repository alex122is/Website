About me

Hi, my name is Alejandro or Alex as many of my friends call me. I am here to provide you with your residential and commerical Hvac needs. I am experiance through hands on practice, a full year as a residential hvac service tech, mastering residential service calls and installations with the help of my mentor. Soon afterwards I joined a company that allowed me to experiance the world of commercial refrigeration as a part time employe for three years.

Now I look forward in providing my services to the community :)

Cost Effective

Contacting me will be a good idea for any homeowner or business individual. I will never overcharge a customer just because my competitors do. I can assure you will get the highest standards of service and installation on your Hvac units.

My prices will always be fair and in most cases on the lower end because I can supplement my income not just by my hvac services. I actually coded this website you're reading :)

Customer Satisfaction

As a certified EPA section 608 member I will make sure the job is done right the first time. Many times, I have witnessed hvac tech's having no clue what they are doing charging the customer wrong parts for their time. Avoid this please, you will be sadly surprised. This happens ALOT!

I will hold any verbal or written warranties accountable as well.
